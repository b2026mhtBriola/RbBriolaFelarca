All Star by Smash Mouth

All star has been used in diverse ways throughout the internet

All Star was first released on May 4, 1999 by the band smash mouth and was a popular song in of itself. Just like most songs and the other songs mentioned, All Star was known for its catchy tune and its catchy lyrics. The lyrics that stand out in particular in this song was "Somebody once told me."

This song was mostly known for its use in the intro of the movie Shrek, which was released on the 18th of May, 2001. This appearance caused the song to be used in a multitude of ways throughout the internet. This included hilarious parodies of the song, memes in the form of pictures and text, witty edits and much more. But there is one YouTuber, Jon Sudano, who ammased an immense amount of attention to this song. He released a cover of this song on the 13th of October, 2016 which received an enormous amount of support for him and the song of All Star.
