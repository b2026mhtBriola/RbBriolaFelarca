Megalovania by Toby Fox

A list of meme songs just isn't complete without some VGM.

Toby Fox's songs are renowned for being incredible works, each tailor-made for the roles they perform in his games. Among the soundtrack of his first hit, Undertale, we find 101 "songs" that reflect every part of the amazing game. Chief among these is Megalovania, the penultimate track of the album.

In-game, the song is the "boss theme" for Sans the Skeleton, a humorous character
