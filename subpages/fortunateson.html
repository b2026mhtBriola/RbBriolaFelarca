Fortunate Son by Creedence Clearwater Revival

All war is bad, but at least Vietnam had a good soudtrack.

In September of 1969, the band CCR released 2 singles: Down on the Corner and Fortunate Son. The latter song's message of distrust and unfairness would come to define its time, as hundreds of thousands of Americans were drafted to fight in the Vietnam War. In their minds, they were being sent off to fight for powerful people that weren't affected by the mess thay made.
