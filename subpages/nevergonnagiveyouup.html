Never Gonna Give You Up

Rick Astley's 1987 hit is widely considered as one of the greatest songs of the '80s. The memorable tune and flashy music video cemented it as a classic song that is hard to forget. It spread rapidly across the Internet as a prank; somebody would click on a link and be transported to the song's YouTube video instead of whatever they intended to visit. Thus began Rick Astley's greatest legacy: the Rickroll.

The Rickroll is widely considered as the greatest meme of all time, and for good reason. First appreaing in 2004, it is a mutated form of the Duckroll, wherein a user clicks a link and is diverted from their intended destination to a photo of a duck on wheels. The first recorded instance of the Rickroll substitited the trailer of Grand Theft Auto 4 for the song.

Since this first instance, the Rickroll has not only cememnted itself as an Internet classic, but it has also caused a resurgence in the popularity of Rick himself, winning the EMA 2008 Best Act of All Time, and other awards.
