Never Gonna Give You Up

Rick Astley's 1987 hit is widely considered as one of the greatest songs of the '80s. The memorable tune and flashy music video cemented it as a classic song that is hard to forget. It spread rapidly across the Internet as a prank; somebody would click on a link and be transported to the song's YouTube video instead of whatever they intended to visit. Thus began Rick Astley's greatest legacy: the Rickroll.
