What is a Meme?

A meme, as defined by Encyclopedia Britannica, is "a unit of cultural information spread through imitation." The concept of the Internet meme, while similar, does not actually reflect this definition. The thing with the Internet meme is that it spreads through replication and mutation. Each iteration of a meme *usually* provides an intentional variation of its original "format".
